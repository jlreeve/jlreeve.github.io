PhD Research at University of Colorado Boulder

Investigating cyanobacterial evolution through microbial experimental evolution. Trying to get to a better of understanding of how/when cyanobacteria evolved, the interaction between their initial evolution and the Great Oxidation Event, and generally understanding mechanisms of evolution.

Masters Research at University of Victoria

Using the dissolved gas tracer N2/Ar and nutrient ratios to investigate nitrogen cycling in the Arctic.

Undergrad Research at Haverford College

Preliminary work on a project investigating degradation of crude oil from the Gulf of Mexico by native fungi isolated from oil soaked sand patties.

Undergrad Research at the Woods Hole Oceanographic Institution

Determining the flow of carbon recently fixed by marsh grass photosynthesis into soil microbial communities.
